About Radio Free Sheboygan

Radio Free Sheboygan is an independent audio project documenting local politics, arts, and culture. We produce long-form interviews, short features, and a rotating photo series. Our mission: preserve local voice and question power.

Shelley Fuchs, Jr.

Host, editor, and interviewer. Shelley brings long-form curiosity and a no-nonsense approach to conversation.

Steve Scotland

Man of many hats and co-founder of Radio Free Sheboygan. Steve focuses on production, concept development, and maintaining the project’s independent spirit.

A dynamic content creator, entrepreneur, and devoted mom of two from Wisconsin. Passionate about luxury, motivation, and lifestyle aesthetics, she builds communities that inspire and uplift—from haircare businesses to interactive social shows like Bakery with Tea.
