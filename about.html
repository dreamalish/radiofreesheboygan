About Radio Free Sheboygan

Radio Free Sheboygan is an independent audio project documenting local politics, arts, and culture. We produce long-form interviews, short features, and a rotating photo series. Our mission: preserve local voice and question power.

Shelley Fuchs, Jr.

Host, editor, and interviewer. Shelley brings long-form curiosity and a no-nonsense approach to conversation.

How we operate

  • Independent funding and editorial control
  • Simple sponsorship model — occasional local underwriting
  • Open transcripts on request
